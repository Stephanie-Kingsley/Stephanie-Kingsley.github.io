M.A. candidate in English, rising publishing and digital humanities professional

Stephanie Kingsley is a second-year Master's student in the University of Virginia English Department. She specializes in 19th-century American literature, textual studies, and digital humanities. Ms. Kingsley is one of this year's Rare Book School Fellows and Praxis Fellows. She is particularly interested in digital applications to textual editing and plans to work in publishing and digital archiving after her graduation in May 2014.
